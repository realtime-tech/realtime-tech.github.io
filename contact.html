Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute to your projects? ‚ñº
All our projects are open source on GitHub! Fork the repository you're interested in, make your changes, and submit a pull request. We welcome contributions of all types - code, documentation, bug reports, or feature suggestions.
Are your tools really free? ‚ñº
Yes! All our tools are 100% free and open source. No hidden costs, no premium tiers, no subscriptions. We believe quality tools should be accessible to everyone.
Do you collect any user data? ‚ñº
No. We don't use analytics, tracking, or any form of data collection. Your privacy is important to us, and all our tools work entirely in your browser without sending any data to external servers.
Can I use your projects commercially? ‚ñº
Yes! Our projects are open source and free to use for any purpose, including commercial use. Check each project's license file for specific terms.
How do I report a bug? ‚ñº
Visit the GitHub repository of the specific project and create an issue. Please include as much detail as possible: what you were doing, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened. Screenshots are always helpful!
Can I request new features? ‚ñº
Absolutely! We love hearing ideas from users. Open an issue on GitHub with your feature request, explain the use case, and we'll discuss it with the community. Better yet, you can implement it yourself and submit a pull request!
